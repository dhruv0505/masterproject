How To Play

You have to guess the word that is generated. You will decide the word length in the beginning. Then you will be asked for a guess that must be the amount of letters you decided. There should not be to letters repeated, you should only use lowercase letters and the words have to be real words, not gibberish. If you look below you can see the cows and belows and type your next guess based on that. Cows are when a letter is correct, but it is in the wrong position, and bulls are where the letter is both correct and in the right position.
